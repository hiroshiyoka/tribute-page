  • UFC 140 - Against Lyoto Machida, win by Technical Submission (Guillotine Choke)

  • UFC 172 - Against Glover Teixeira, win by Decision
  • UFC 182 - Against Daniel Cormier, win by Decision

  • UFC 214 - Against Daniel Cormier (2), Uncontest (overtuned by CSAC)
  • UFC 232 - Against Alexander Gustafsson (2), win by KO
  • UFC 235 - Against Anthony Smith, win by Decision
